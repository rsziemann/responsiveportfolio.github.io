Thank you for coming to my portfolio Website. A little bit about me, well as you can see from my photos I'm a greyhound. Oh did I forget to mention that I used to be a Race dog down in Oklahoma. Lucky for me I wasn't very fast and they retired me early thats when I met my human and went to my forever home. Since that day four years ago I have lived the life. It is my mission now to help other retired race dogs find forever homes.
